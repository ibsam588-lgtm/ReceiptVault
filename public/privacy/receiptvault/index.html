ReceiptVault Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 5, 2026

ReceiptVault helps users store receipt images, track simple expenses, and manage return or warranty dates.

Information You Provide

ReceiptVault may store receipt photos, store names, purchase dates, purchase amounts, categories, return dates, warranty dates, and notes that you add or confirm in the app.

Connected Email Accounts

If you choose to connect an email account, ReceiptVault uses that account only to find receipt, order, invoice, purchase confirmation, return, or warranty messages. ReceiptVault does not use connected email accounts to read or store unrelated personal messages. Messages that do not appear to be receipts or orders are discarded and are not saved in your vault.

You may connect multiple supported accounts, such as Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, or other supported mail accounts. Each account can be disconnected, and imported receipt data can be deleted from ReceiptVault.

Local Storage

By default, receipt information is stored locally on your device in private app storage.

Optional Cloud Backup

Paid backup features may upload receipt images and receipt metadata to Corsair Labs cloud storage so users can restore their vault. Cloud backup is optional and is only used when enabled by the user.

OCR

ReceiptVault uses text recognition to read receipt text and suggest store, date, amount, and category labels.

AI Categorization

ReceiptVault may send receipt or order text to Gemini 2.5 Flash-Lite through Corsair Labs servers to suggest categories, warranty candidates, return windows, merchant names, and totals. ReceiptVault should not send unrelated email content for AI categorization.

Data Sharing

ReceiptVault may use service providers such as Google, Firebase, and Cloudflare to run OCR, authentication, AI categorization, billing, and optional cloud backup. ReceiptVault does not sell personal or sensitive user data.

Retention and Deletion

Locally stored receipts remain on your device until you delete them or uninstall the app. Optional cloud backup data remains until you delete the receipt, disable backup and request deletion, or close your ReceiptVault account.

Payments

Paid app subscriptions are processed by Google Play Billing. ReceiptVault does not store full payment card details.
