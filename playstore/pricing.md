# ReceiptVault Pricing Model

## Free

Price: $0

- 50 stored receipts.
- 5 warranty items.
- Camera scan.
- Image upload.
- Email image import through Android Share.
- Basic OCR.
- Basic search.

## ReceiptVault Plus

Price: $4.99/month or $39.99/year

- 1,000 stored receipts.
- Optional Cloudflare R2 cloud backup.
- Restore backed-up receipts on a new device.
- Unlimited warranty tracking.
- Return and warranty reminders.
- Advanced search.
- CSV/PDF export.

## Later Pro Plan

Price target: $9.99/month or $79.99/year

- Higher receipt limit.
- Business folders.
- Tax/export reports.
- Priority support.

Google Play Billing product IDs should be created after the Play Console app is
created. Suggested IDs:

- `receiptvault_plus_monthly`
- `receiptvault_plus_yearly`
- `receiptvault_pro_monthly`
- `receiptvault_pro_yearly`
