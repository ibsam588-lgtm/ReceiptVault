## Title

ReceiptVault

## Short Description

Scan receipts, track expenses, and never lose warranty proof again.

## Full Description

ReceiptVault helps you store receipts, find proof of purchase, and track
warranties without turning your life into accounting homework.

Take a photo of a receipt or upload a receipt image from your gallery, files, or
email app. ReceiptVault uses OCR to read the store name, purchase date, total,
and category, then saves the original receipt image in your vault.

Use ReceiptVault to:

- Save receipt images for returns and warranty claims.
- Search receipts by store, date, category, or detected receipt text.
- Track simple monthly expenses.
- Keep return dates and warranty dates in one place.
- Import receipt images from email apps using Android Share.

ReceiptVault is built for individuals, families, freelancers, and small business
owners who want a simple proof-of-purchase system without a complicated
bookkeeping app.
