Privacy Policy

ReceiptVault  ·  Effective June 10, 2025

We built ReceiptVault to help you track your purchases — not to collect your data. This policy explains exactly what we access, what we store, and how you stay in control.

1. Who We Are

ReceiptVault is developed and operated by Corsair Labs. Questions about this policy can be sent to ibsam588@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

Account information. When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and display name via Firebase Authentication. We do not store your Google password.

Email metadata (when you connect Gmail). If you choose to connect a Gmail account for receipt import, we request the gmail.readonly permission. We use this to scan your inbox for emails that look like receipts, orders, or invoices. Specifically, we access:

  • Email subject line
  • Sender name and address (From header)
  • Email date
  • Email snippet / preview text
  • Message body — accessed temporarily in memory only to extract merchant name and purchase amount

What we do NOT store. Email body content is processed in memory on our server and immediately discarded. We do not store, log, or retain the text of your emails. We never read emails unrelated to purchases.

Receipt records. When a receipt is imported or manually added, we store structured receipt data in your account: merchant name, purchase amount, date, category, and any notes you add. This data is associated with your user ID.

OAuth tokens. To sync your Gmail account we store a refresh token, encrypted with AES-256-GCM, in your private storage bucket. The encryption key is never stored alongside the token.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Authenticate you and maintain your account
  • Scan your connected email accounts for receipt and order emails
  • Extract and display receipt data (merchant, amount, date) in the app
  • Store your receipt history so you can access it across devices

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the receipt management features you have enabled. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

4. Gmail Data — Google API Policy Compliance

ReceiptVault's use of Gmail data complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Specifically:

  • We only request gmail.readonly access, the minimum scope needed
  • Gmail data is used only to import your receipts — for no other purpose
  • We do not transfer Gmail data to third parties except as necessary to provide the service
  • We do not use Gmail data to serve advertisements
  • Email message body content is never persisted to disk or storage

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Cloudflare R2 object storage, isolated per user account. OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM before storage. Cloudflare applies additional storage-layer encryption. All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS.

Access to your data requires a valid Firebase authentication token. We do not share bucket access with any third party.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

  • Disconnect an email account — removes the OAuth token and stops future syncs immediately
  • Delete a receipt — permanently removes that record from our storage
  • Delete your account — contact us at ibsam588@gmail.com and we will delete all data associated with your user ID within 30 days

7. Third-Party Services

ReceiptVault uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google) — user sign-in. Privacy policy
  • Cloudflare R2 — encrypted data storage. Privacy policy
  • Google Gmail API — email receipt scanning (only when you enable it). Privacy policy

8. Children's Privacy

ReceiptVault is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
